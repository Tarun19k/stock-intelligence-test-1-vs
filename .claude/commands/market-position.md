# Market Positioning — GSI Dashboard

Competitive analysis, messaging, and positioning for public launch.

## One-line positioning
"The free, no-login multi-market stock signal dashboard that combines Weinstein Stage analysis
and Elder Triple Screen into a single verdict — covering India, USA, Europe, China and more."

## Competitive landscape

| Tool | Strength | Weakness | GSI advantage |
|---|---|---|---|
| TradingView | Best charting, huge community | Free = 2 indicators, constant upsell ($15+/mo) | Fully free, no paywall |
| Yahoo Finance | Fundamental data, news | Poor signal aggregation, US-centric | Multi-market signals, India-first |
| Screener.in | Indian fundamentals | India only, no technical signals | 9 markets, technical + fundamental |
| Ticker Tape | India UX | India only, subscription for advanced | Free, global coverage |
| Bloomberg | Comprehensive | $30k/year | Free |

## Key differentiators (lead with these)
1. **Free with no login** — instant access, no account required
2. **India-first, globally aware** — deep NSE/BSE coverage + 8 other markets
3. **Two-method signal arbitration** — Weinstein Stage + Elder Triple Screen → one verdict
4. **Educational transparency** — every signal explained in plain English with HELP tooltips
5. **Open source** — code visible on GitHub, no black box

## What NOT to claim (legal red lines)
- Never: "investment advice", "buy/sell recommendations", "guaranteed signals"
- Never: "SEBI-registered" (not registered)
- Never: "real-time data" (yfinance has 15–20min delay for most markets)
- Always: "educational tool", "signal visualisation", "not investment advice"
