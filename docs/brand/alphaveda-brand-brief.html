AlphaVeda
"Know before you act." Ancient wisdom. Modern signals.

AlphaVeda is a personal portfolio analytics platform for Indian retail investors who want to move beyond gut-feel decisions and into data-driven market understanding. By synthesising technical signals, fundamental indicators, and macro patterns across NSE and BSE-listed equities, AlphaVeda surfaces the patterns in your portfolio and the market — giving you analytical clarity to ask better questions and make more informed choices on your own. AlphaVeda illuminates the data. You drive the decision.

Logo Concept Directions

Recommended ✦
Concept A — The Yantra Mark
Simplified geometric form from the Sri Yantra — concentric triangles reduced to 2–3 clean lines. Upward-pointing triangle signals alpha (above benchmark). Wordmark in Fraunces beside it. Timeless, scalable, India-rooted without being ornate. Recommended for launch.
Recommended ✦
α
Concept B — The Alpha-Akshara
Custom letterform merging the Greek alpha symbol (α) with Devanagari stroke style — thick-thin contrast, calligraphic curve at the terminal. Reads as both "α" and as something rooted in Indian script tradition. Most distinctive. Best if custom typeface budget is available.
Concept C — The Constellation Mark
Five dots in asymmetric pattern (star cluster) connected by thin lines — signal nodes in a market intelligence network. Dots can animate in the digital product. Less India-specific but universally legible at small sizes. Best if international expansion is planned.

Brand Personality

Precise
Lead ★
Language is exact. Data is presented with source, timeframe, and methodology visible. Nothing is vague. This is the non-negotiable foundation.
✓"RSI trend sustained above 60 across 8 of last 12 months."
✗"This stock looks strong right now."
Calm
Never alarms, hypes, or reacts. The opposite of financial Twitter. The market moves — AlphaVeda holds steady.
✓"Volatility elevated vs. 90-day baseline. Portfolio exposure: 18%."
✗"ALERT: Markets crashing! Sell now!"
Deeply Indian
NSE/BSE tickers, Nifty/Sensex benchmarks, rupee denomination throughout. No US defaults. Respects Indian market realities: circuit filters, T+1 settlement, F&O rollover.
✓"Benchmark: Nifty 500 TRI (dividends included)."
✗"Benchmark: S&P 500 equivalent."
Empowering
Puts analytical power in the user's hands. Never tells you what to do. Tells you what the data says — so you can decide. SEBI compliance is built into this attribute.
✓"Three indicators are aligned positively as of today's close."
✗"This is a buy signal."
Rooted in Knowledge
The Veda dimension is not decorative. Market intelligence as a discipline — a structured body of knowledge that compounds over time, like learning itself.
✓"Pattern recognition improves as your historical data deepens."
✗"Premium users get more signals."

P1 Say what the data shows — not what it means for the user's decision
✓ AlphaVeda says
→"Price momentum (6-month) is in the top quartile of this index."
→"Promoter holding has declined 4.2% in the last two quarters."
✗ AlphaVeda doesn't say
→"Now is a good time to add to this position."
→"Watch out — insiders may be exiting."
P2 Plain language over jargon — but never dumbed down
✓ AlphaVeda says
→"EV/EBITDA: 11.2x — common multiple for capital-intensive businesses."
→"Debt-to-equity: 0.8 (sector range: 0.3–1.4)."
✗ AlphaVeda doesn't say
→"Enterprise Value to EBITDA equals 11.2."
→"D/E ratio is moderate. Be careful."
P3 Confident but never certain — probabilistic framing always
✓ AlphaVeda says
→"In 7 of the last 10 comparable macro environments, this sector saw compressed margins."
→"Pattern resembles Q3 FY22 setup — context differs, but overlap is notable."
✗ AlphaVeda doesn't say
→"Margins will compress this quarter."
→"This is exactly what happened in 2022."
