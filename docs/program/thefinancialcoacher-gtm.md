## SECTION 1: ManyChat vs Alternatives

### 1.1 ManyChat Pricing (post-March 2026 restructure)

ManyChat overhauled pricing on March 2, 2026 — four tiers, free plan cut from 1,000 contacts to 25.

| Plan | Monthly Price | Contacts | Key Features |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | 25 | 2 channels (Instagram + FB Messenger), basic keyword triggers, comment-to-DM |
| Essential | $14/mo | 250 | 2 channels, basic sequences, no A/B testing |
| Pro | $29/mo | 2,500 | 3 channels (adds WhatsApp/SMS), A/B testing, sequences, broadcasts |
| Business | $69/mo | 7,500 | All Pro + priority support |
| AI Add-on | +$29/mo | — | AI-powered DM replies; Pro or Business only |

Overage: $0.018–$0.10 per contact above plan limit. WhatsApp: $0.02–$0.08 per conversation on top of subscription. Free tier (25 contacts) is a sandbox only — not viable for a live launch.

---

### 1.2 Top 3 Alternatives

**LinkDM**
- Pricing: Free (1,000 auto DMs/month) · Pro $19/mo
- Instagram DM support: Native, certified Meta Business Partner
- Ease of use: 4.5/5 — creator-focused, minimal setup
- Differentiator: Most generous genuine free tier. Instagram-only (no WhatsApp or multi-channel).

**Chatfuel (Fuely AI)**
- Pricing: No permanent free tier (7-day trial). Fuely Super starts at $39/mo for 150 contacts.
- Instagram DM support: Yes, plus WhatsApp, TikTok, website widget
- Ease of use: 3/5 — AI-first, better for FAQ bots than creator funnels
- Differentiator: GPT-class AI built in. Expensive for a solo creator at launch.

**InstantDM**
- Pricing: Free tier available; paid plans ~$15–25/mo
- Instagram DM support: Yes — Meta Business Partner, comment-to-DM, story reply, broadcast
- Ease of use: 4/5 — clean UI, Instagram-focused, cleaner analytics than LinkDM

---

### 1.3 Recommendation

**Start with LinkDM Free. Upgrade to ManyChat Pro ($29/mo) when DM volume exceeds 500 conversations/month.**

- At <1,000 followers, DM volume is low. LinkDM's free tier (1,000 DMs/month) covers the entire 21-day sprint at zero cost.
- ManyChat's free tier (25 contacts) is too restrictive to test real funnels.
- Upgrade trigger is clear: when comment-to-DM funnels generate >500 unique conversations/month, ManyChat Pro unlocks broadcasts, sequences, and multi-channel.
- LinkDM's Meta Business Partner certification means no shadowban risk on a brand-new account.

🔴 **DECISION: Is WhatsApp part of the DM funnel?** If yes, ManyChat Pro ($29/mo) from day 1 — LinkDM is Instagram-only.

## SECTION 4: Content Templates & Scripts

### 4.1 Reel Script Template (60-second)

```
[0–3 sec — HOOK: pattern interrupt, bold claim, or question]
"Most people in India are doing this one thing wrong with their money."

[3–8 sec — CREDIBILITY BRIDGE]
"I've tracked 559 stocks across 9 markets. I see this mistake everywhere."

[8–20 sec — PROBLEM: name the pain specifically]
"They invest money they can't afford to lock away. Market drops — they panic sell.
Not because of the market. Because of their cash flow structure."

[20–45 sec — SOLUTION: 2–3 specific, actionable steps]
"Step 1: Build a 3-month emergency fund in a liquid fund — not your savings account.
Step 2: Only invest money you won't need for at least 3 years.
Step 3: Automate your SIP so the decision is already made before the market moves."

[45–55 sec — PROOF / SOCIAL PROOF]
"This is exactly what I walked a client through last month. Their portfolio stopped
being a source of anxiety and became a system."

[55–60 sec — CTA: specific, low-friction]
"Comment 'SYSTEM' below and I'll send you the exact cash flow framework I use."
```

Production notes: Subtitles on. Low/no background music. Face on camera for first 3 seconds. Hook text as overlay. End frame: account handle + offer.

---

### 4.2 Carousel Slide Template (7 slides)

**Slide 1 — Cover:** Bold headline (max 8 words). Subheadline optional: "Swipe before your next trade." Brand colours + logo. No clutter.

**Slide 2 — Setup:** One sentence framing the problem: "Most investors skip these. It costs them." 1–2 lines of context.

**Slides 3–6 — Content (one point each):** Number + bold title. 2–3 lines max. One visual element (icon, chart, formula). Reading level: Persona 1 (Priya).

**Slide 7 — CTA:** "Save this for your next trade review" / "Comment NUMBERS below" / @thefinancialcoacher.

Design note: Consistent fonts and colours across all slides (Canva templates). Each carousel should take <30 min once template is set.

---

### 4.3 Story Sequence Template (3 frames)

**Frame 1 — Tease:** Text on solid background or selfie video. "I found something interesting in the data today..." Poll or emoji slider optional. CTA: "Tap right to see it."

**Frame 2 — Value:** Screen recording, chart screenshot, or carousel slide preview. The actual insight in 2–4 short lines. Optional: "Does this apply to you? Yes / Not sure" poll.

**Frame 3 — CTA:** Clean text or face-to-camera. "If this resonated — two options: (1) DM me 'COACH' for the free guide / (2) Link in bio for the full framework." Link sticker where available.
