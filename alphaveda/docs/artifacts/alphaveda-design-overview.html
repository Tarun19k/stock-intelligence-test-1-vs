Data and Trust Pipeline

The design makes backend trust visible: market sources flow into ingest checks, database records, signal generation, prediction grading, and the public scorecard.

01

Market sources

NSE/BSE end-of-day prices, fundamentals, and macro context.

02

Ingest checks

Normalize rows, detect staleness, and flag circuit-locked prices.

03

Signal engine

Arbitrate evidence, calibrate confidence, and suppress weak calls.

04

Ledger

Record predictions and grade outcomes after the defined horizon.

05

Accuracy UI

Expose scorecard evidence, cold-start counts, and review status.

Important Product States

Non-happy paths are designed as trust signals. AlphaVeda should explain missing data, stale feeds, cold starts, and no-call decisions clearly.

Empty

No data yet

The data source is still being set up. Do not show silent blanks.

Stale

Data delayed

Signals may be limited until the next successful ingest completes.

Cold

Too early

The segment needs more graded results before strong confidence is shown.

No call

No opinion today

Insufficient or conflicting evidence makes silence the safer output.
